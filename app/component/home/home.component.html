" She's Better After it All " Is not just a concept, it is not just an idea or a saying that sounds good enough to set as a statues or share for a quick emotional bandage for a wound. It is the reality of a lifestyle that we must take on and make our our own it is the peices that have been picked up not by choice but because they where the only things left of a life we once knew. It is the truth that Life does not come wrapped with a bow and tie. It Does not come with all the bells and whistles as we would like it too. Often it comes with bumps and bruise, many that will change our lives for the better or the worse. Finding the strength that is needed to pick up these peices and start a new once again after it has all fallen apart. It is by no means simple or easy and every broken peice bears a scare of its own. For we all have our fair share of challenges no one is exempt. Life throws you to and frow like a boat on the sea and your left without a sense of direction or a sense of being. When the pain is no longer their and you've had to become a big girl and whipe all your tears. Years down the line you realize she'e not broken she's not dead. You Realize SHEBA She's Better After It all.
